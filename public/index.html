      Baconizer

      Welcome to Baconizer, a web game based on The Six Degrees of Kevin Bacon! Enter a start actor and an end actor and try to find the shortest path between them by jumping between movies and actors. Try starting with Kevin Bacon as a nod to the original game or randomize the actors for an extra challenge!

      Try dragging and zooming the graph to help locate the next movie or actor in the path.

      There are over 100,000 actors in our database, all of whom have a base level of popularity and at least 2 movie credits to their name.
